    Request Help

    This section has two main parts to it: Request help from Admin and the FAQ. Our administrators are here to help you with any questions or issues you may have while using the platform. Whether it's troubleshooting technical problems, clarifying platform features, or providing general assistance, we're dedicated to ensuring your experience is smooth and enjoyable.

    Request help from admin

    If you encounter any issues or have questions while using the platform, you can easily request help from one of our administrators. Simply navigate to the "Request Help" section, where you'll find a form to fill in with details about your query.

    Step 1: Submitting Your Query:

    In the form, you'll be prompted to provide information such as your name, email address, and a description of the issue or question you have. Be sure to include as much detail as possible to help our administrators understand your query and provide the best possible assistance.

    Step 2: Sending Your Request:

    Once you've filled in the form, simply submit it, and your query will be sent directly to one of our administrators. They will receive your request and review the details you provided.

    Step 3: Admin Response:

    Our administrators are committed to providing timely assistance, and they will reply to you via email to address your query. You can expect to receive a response to your email address within a reasonable timeframe, typically within a week.

    Step 4: Email Communication:

    The administrator's response will be sent to the email address you provided in the form. Be sure to check your inbox (and spam/junk folder, just in case) for their reply. You can then respond directly to the email to continue the conversation and resolve your query.
