New User Guide

If you select the "New User" option, it implies that you are new to trading or cryptocurrency platforms. Here's what you can expect:

  1. Beginner-Friendly Features: The platform offers simplified features and interfaces tailored for users with limited experience in trading or investing.
  2. Educational Resources: There are educational resources such as tutorials, guides, and FAQs to help users understand the basics of trading, investment strategies, and how to use the platform effectively.
  3. Simple market analysis As a new user you will have access to straightforward market analysis tools and information. This analysis might include basic charts, price trends, and simple indicators to help users understand market movements.
  4. Support and Assistance: There's dedicated customer support available to assist new users with any questions or issues you encounter while navigating the platform.

Traits you may have

  • Limited experience in trading or investing.
  • Seeking educational resources and guidance to understand trading concepts.
  • Prefer straightforward and easy-to-use features.
  • Looking for basic market analysis to make informed decisions.
